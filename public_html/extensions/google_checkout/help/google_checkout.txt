This manual is to help you configure extension and Google Checkout service
<br/><br/>

1. Go to http://www.google.com/checkout and create merchant account.
<br/><br/>

2. Once you are subscribed, You will need to go to settings and fill your profile.<br/>
Note: you need to set Public business website field by URL like http://YOUR_DOMAIN/index.php?rt=checkout/success to direct customer on success page after buying.
<br/><br/>

3. Then go to https://www.google.com/checkout/sell/settings?section=Integration  and set callback url as<br/>
https://YOUR_DOMAIN/index.php?rt=extension/google_checkout/callback
<b>Note:</b> You need to turn on SSL in your AbanteCart settings.  <br/><br/>

4. Go to your Google Checkout Extension edit page and fill all fields by given Merchant ID and Key from integration settings page of Google checkout.
<br/><br/>


5. If you need to test extension with google checkout sandbox go to https://developers.google.com/checkout/developer/Google_Checkout_Basic_HTML_Sandbox#Locate_ID_and_Key
 and follow steps.
